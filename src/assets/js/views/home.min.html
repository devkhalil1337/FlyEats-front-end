About us

Welcome to our Website! We are here to turn your dull moments golden through our amazing online services. We don’t just cater to your taste bud, we cater to you. We have developed this website for you, our valued patron, to enable breezy smooth access to our takeaway. It is complete with our most updated menu for you to choose from click. Select. Order. It’s really that straightforward! What’s more, for your convenience we offer flexible payment options .i.e., you could either pay right away using your card or pay on delivery. Unlike other businesses, we let you track your order in real time so that you know the exact status of your order (which stage it is at, delivery time, etc). You don’t have to select and order from scratch each time you wish to place an order. Using the ‘order history’, just view your previous order(s) and select the one (if it’s the same thing you are looking for) you like to have; order and that’s it!
